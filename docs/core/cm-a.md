# CM-A

CM-A is the first core movement sequence.

## Purpose

Teach the basic relationship between target attacks and matching parries.

## Notation

| Player | Step 1 | Step 2 | Step 3 | Step 4 | Step 5 | Step 6 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Lumen A | 1 | 11 | 3 | 9 | 5 | 7 |
| Lumen B | 1P | 11P | 3P | 9P | 5P | 7P |

## Training notes

- Practice slowly first.
- Confirm both partners understand the target lines.
- Do not speed up until the exchange is clean and safe.
