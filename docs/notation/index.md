# LUMINA Notation

LUMINA Notation is the written movement language of the LUMINA Standard.

It records choreographed saber movement using intended target points, defensive responses, and supporting movement symbols.

## Basic format

A simple LUMINA sequence is written as a two-row table:

| Player | Step 1 | Step 2 | Step 3 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Lumen A | 1 | 11 | 3 |
| Lumen B | 1P | 11P | 3P |

A number shows the target of an attack.  
A number followed by `P` shows the matching parry.
